Training Packages

Your roadmap starts here

After years of experience in training dogs of all temperaments and abilities, my most successful clients have one thing in common - the ability to remain consistent & persistent in applying their knowledge.

As our dogs' human companions, we have an enormous impact on their daily lives. It's one thing to achieve your goals in session, but keeping consistent & persistent in both applying the techniques learned and understanding what those techniques say about us as humans is key to becoming a true advocate for your dog. By being more purposeful and mindful in our actions, we can meet the needs of our dogs and positively impact their perception of their surroundings and their people.

The training packages below offer hands-on training, spaced repetition (a proven method for retaining information longer), and more opportunities for building confidence as you progress. Every package includes a complimentary phone consultation, where depending on the complexity of your case I will recommend a training plan, or you can create your own.
